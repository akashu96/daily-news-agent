## 🗺️ BEFORE WE BEGIN — A Quick Glossary for You

Think of the **Nifty 50** like a report card for India's top 50 companies. When Nifty goes up, India's biggest businesses are doing well. When it falls, there's worry in the air.

- **Support** = A floor price — where buyers usually show up and stop the fall.
- **Resistance** = A ceiling price — where sellers usually take profits and slow the rise.
- **RSI** = A "temperature gauge" for the market. Above 70 = too hot (might cool down). Below 30 = too cold (might bounce up).
- **FII** = Foreign big money investors. **DII** = Indian mutual funds & banks.
- **EMA/SMA** = Averages of past prices — used to see the trend direction.
