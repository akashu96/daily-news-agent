## 🤔 First, A Quick Explainer for New Investors
Think of the **Nifty 50** like the "report card" of India's top 50 companies — big names like Reliance, HDFC Bank, Infosys, TCS, etc. When these companies do well, the index goes up. When they struggle, it falls. It's the single best number to understand how India's stock market is doing on any given day.
