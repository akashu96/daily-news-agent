## 🧭 WHAT IS NIFTY 50? (For the First-Time Reader)

Think of Nifty 50 as a **report card for India's 50 biggest companies** — like HDFC Bank, Reliance, TCS, Infosys, and Bajaj Finance — all rolled into one number. 

It is India's main benchmark stock market index, representing the top 50 large and liquid companies listed on the National Stock Exchange (NSE) across key sectors like IT, banking, FMCG, energy, and more.

 When this number goes up, it generally means Indian businesses are doing well. When it falls, it signals caution.
