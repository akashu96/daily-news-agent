## 🧭 QUICK CONTEXT FIRST — What is Nifty 50?

Think of the **Nifty 50** like a report card for India's 50 biggest and most important companies. When Nifty goes up, it generally means big Indian businesses are doing well. When it falls, something is worrying investors. It's the most watched stock market indicator in India — like a health thermometer for the economy.
