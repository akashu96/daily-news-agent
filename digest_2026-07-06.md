## 🤔 What Even Is Nifty 50? (For First-Timers!)

Think of **Nifty 50** as a "report card" for India's stock market. It tracks the **50 biggest and most important companies** listed on India's National Stock Exchange (NSE) — companies like Reliance, TCS, HDFC Bank, Infosys, etc. When Nifty goes **up**, it means these big companies are doing well. When it goes **down**, there's worry in the air. Simple as that!
